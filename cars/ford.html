Ford Mustang GT-500 1968

  • Engine: 428 V8
  • Horse Power: 355 hp
  • Torque: 420 ft-lb
  • 0-60 MPH: 6.5 seconds

The 1968 Ford Mustang GT500 was a high-performance version of the popularFord Mustang. It featured a 428 cubic inch V8 engine that produced 355 horsepower and could accelerate from 0-60 mph in around 6 seconds. .

It also had a 4-speed manual transmission and a top speed of around 130 mph. The GT500 was available as a coupe or convertible and had a distinctive "GT" grille and side stripes. The 1968 model was the first year of the GT500 and it was considered one of the most powerful and capable Mustangs of its time

Ford Torino Cobra 1969

  • Engine: 428-4V FE V8
  • Horse Power: 335 hp
  • Torque: 445 lb-ft
  • 0-60 MPH: 6.3 seconds

The Ford Torino Cobra was a high-performance version of the Ford Torino, produced by Ford in 1969. It was available in both hardtop and convertible body styles, and was powered by a variety of V8 engines, including the 428 Cobra Jet and the 429 Super Cobra Jet.

The Torino Cobra was known for its aggressive styling, and was also available with a variety of performance options, such as a "drag pack" that included heavy-duty suspension and brakes. The 1969 model year was the only year that the Torino Cobra was produced, making it a highly sought-after collector's item today.

Ford Mustang 2015

  • Engine: 5.0-liter V8
  • Horse Power: 435 hp
  • Torque: 400 lb-ft
  • 0-60 MPH: 4.4 seconds

The 2015 Mustang is a sports car produced by Ford. It was the sixth generation of the Mustang model, and was available in fastback and convertible body styles. The 2015 Mustang was the first model to feature an independent rear suspension, which improved handling and ride comfort. It was also the first Mustang to be sold globally.

The 2015 Mustang was offered with three engine options. The base engine is a 3.7-liter V6 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The next option is a 2.3-liter EcoBoost four-cylinder engine that produces 310 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque. The top engine option is a 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces 435 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque.

The exterior design of the 2015 Mustang was heavily influenced by the Mustang models of the 1960s, with a long hood and short rear deck, and familiar design cues such as the three-bar taillights. The interior was also updated with a more modern and premium feel, with higher-quality materials used throughout.

The 2015 Mustang received positive reviews for its improved handling and ride, as well as its updated design and features. It continues to be a popular choice among Mustang enthusiasts and car buyers looking for a powerful and stylish sports car.
