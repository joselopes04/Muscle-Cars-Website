Plymouth Barracuda 1972

  • Engine: 6.3 L V8
  • Horse Power: 335 hp
  • Torque: 410 ft-lb
  • 0-60 MPH: 7.0 seconds

The 1972 Plymouth Barracuda was a pony car produced by Plymouth. It was available as a two-door coupe or convertible and was offered in a variety of trim levels, including the base model, Gran Coupe, and 'Cuda. The Barracuda shared its E-body platform with the Dodge Challenger.

In 1972, the Barracuda was available with a variety of engines, including a 225-cubic-inch (3.7 L) slant-six, a 318-cubic-inch (5.2 L) V8, a 340-cubic-inch (5.6 L) V8, and a 383-cubic-inch (6.3 L) V8. The 'Cuda version was powered by a standard 335 horsepower 400 cid V8 engine. The car had a 0-60 mph acceleration time of around 7.0 seconds.

The 1972 model year was the final year for the second generation Barracuda, which was in production from 1970 to 1974.

The 'Cuda had a distinctive design with a long hood and short deck, and it featured a variety of cosmetic upgrades such as 'Cuda badges and emblems, special wheel covers, and a blacked-out grille.

Plymouth Hemi'cuda 1970

  • Engine: 7.0 L Hemi V8
  • Horse Power: 425 hp
  • Torque: 490 lb-ft
  • 0-60 MPH: 4.5 seconds

The 1970 Plymouth Hemi 'Cuda was a muscle car produced by Plymouth as a high-performance version of the Barracuda. The Hemi 'Cuda was available as a hardtop coupe and was only produced in small numbers.

The 1970 Hemi 'Cuda was powered by a 426 cubic inches (7.0 L) Hemi V8 engine that produced 425 horsepower and 490 lb-ft of torque. It featured a four-speed manual or three-speed automatic transmission. The car had a 0-60 mph acceleration time of around 4.5 seconds.

The 1970 Hemi 'Cuda featured a sport-tuned suspension, front disc brakes, and a variety of cosmetic upgrades such as Hemi 'Cuda badges and emblems, special wheel covers, and a blacked-out grille. It also had a variety of advanced features for the time, such as power windows, power locks, and air conditioning. The Hemi 'Cuda was considered one of the most powerful muscle cars of its time, and it is highly sought after by collectors today.

  • Engine: Hemi V8

  • 0-60 MPH: 4.5 seconds
